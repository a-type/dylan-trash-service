A Few More Things to Know:

In the case of a holiday weekend, or dangerous weather, service may be rescheduled. You will be notified via email in advance as much as possible.

Payment will be collected on the last day of the previous month. That means that your payment for February would be collected on January 31st.

Payment is pro-rated for the first month. Your first payment will be collected along with your $30 trash can deposit when the trash can is delivered to you.
